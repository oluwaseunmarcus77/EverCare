About Us

EverCare Home Health offers a full range of in-home care services to seniors and adults with disabilities in the Chicagoland area. Our highly trained homemakers understand that you need quality care for your loved ones, so we work with you and your loved one to create a care plan based on their specific needs and assign a homemaker to fulfill that plan. We are structured to provide specific levels of senior home care services for your loved one. Our elderly in-home care assistance is an alternative to an assisted living facility or nursing home. This allows your loved one to enjoy independence and the comfort of home.

Our Services

At our agency, we pride ourselves on offering a wide range of tailored home care services to ensure seniors receive the attention, care, and respect they deserve. From specialized medical services to personal care, our team is dedicated to promoting health, comfort, and independence for every client.

In-Home Nursing

Our in-home nursing services provide a reliable and professional solution for seniors requiring medical attention at home. From wound care, IV therapy, and health assessments to post-surgical support and chronic disease management, our licensed nurses are trained to deliver compassionate and expert care. With us, you can rest assured that your loved ones receive the highest standard of medical support without leaving the comfort of their home.

Physical Therapy

Recovering from surgery or coping with a chronic condition can be challenging. That’s why our personalized physical therapy services are designed to help seniors regain their mobility, strength, and confidence. Our licensed therapists work with each individual to develop a customized therapy plan focused on restoring independence and improving overall well-being. Whether it’s balance improvement, joint flexibility, or managing pain, our team is here to guide every step of the way.

Personal Care Assistance

Everyday activities such as bathing, grooming, dressing, and meal preparation can become overwhelming for seniors. Our personal care assistance services provide the help they need to maintain their independence and dignity while staying safe. Our caregivers are trained to offer compassionate, respectful, and personalized support that helps seniors live comfortably. From companionship to light housekeeping, we ensure every day is made easier and brighter for your loved ones.
